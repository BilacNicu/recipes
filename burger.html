Flat Loaf Bread Burger

Ingredients:

  • Flat Loaf Bread
  • Beef Patty
  • Lettuce
  • Cheddar
  • Lard
  • Mujdei
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Bacon
  • French Fries

Prepping:

  1. Cut the loaf in half across the middle
  2. Cut the tomatoes, lettuce accordingly
  3. Fry the frenchies and bacon
  4. Prepare the mujdei using heavy cream, garlic, salt and sparkling water
  5. Flatten the patties and cook them on the barbecue, but not for long
  6. Put some latd on the inside of the loaf and toast them on the barbecue
  7. Line the loaf with mujdei on both sides
  8. Place the patty first, then the cheddar, lettuce, tomato and bacon
  9. Serve with fries on the side
