Guiaș

Ingredients:

  • Potatoes
  • Bell Pepper
  • Hot Pepper
  • Tomato Juice
  • Tomato Paste from Concentrate
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Dafin Leaves
  • Paprika
  • Cooking oil/Lard (not too much)
  • Pork Muscle
  • Sausage
  • Ceafa

Prepping:

  1. Wait until oil is bubbling, then add Onions
  2. After onions have browned a bit, add the peppers
  3. When the peppers have softened, add the meat
  4. Also add the tomato sauce and concentrate
  5. Next add a couple potatoes
  6. After 15-20 mins you can add some water, the condiments and the rest of the potatoes
  7. Wait until the desired consistency and serve
